Chapter 1: The definition and traits of the toxic woman.

In this chapter, I will explain what a toxic woman is and how she differs from a normal woman. I will also show you the characteristics of a toxic woman and how she behaves with her partner. Finally, I will introduce you to the types of toxic women that exist and how to identify them.

- What is a toxic woman and how is she different from a normal woman?

A toxic woman is one who has a dysfunctional, selfish, narcissistic, dependent or aggressive personality, and who uses different strategies to subject her partner to her will, such as lying, emotional blackmail, guilt, threats, contempt or violence.

A toxic woman does not respect her partner, does not value him, does not support him, does not listen to him, does not understand him, does not give him space, does not let him grow, does not make him happy. On the contrary, she makes him feel bad, manipulates him, criticizes him, controls him, isolates him, humiliates him, mistreats him.

A normal woman is one who has a healthy, balanced, empathetic, independent or cooperative personality, and who uses different resources to communicate with her partner, such as truth, respect, responsibility, dialogue, affection or support.

A normal woman respects her partner, values him, supports him, listens to him, understands him, gives him space, lets him grow, makes him happy. At the same time, she respects herself, values herself, supports herself, listens to herself, understands herself, gives herself space, lets herself grow, makes herself happy.

- What are the characteristics of a toxic woman and how does she behave with her partner?

A toxic woman has the following characteristics:

Has low self-esteem and great insecurity. She feels inferior to others and constantly needs her partner's approval and attention. She compares herself to other women and feels threatened by them.

has a high level of demand and perfectionism. She wants her partner to meet all his expectations and demands. Does not tolerate mistakes or discrepancies. She is easily frustrated when things do not go as she wants.

Has a distorted sense of reality. Sees things from his or her subjective point of view and does not accept other perspectives. Has irrational and inflexible beliefs about how the relationship should be and how his or her partner should behave.

Has a negative and ineffective communication style. Does not know how to express feelings and needs in a clear and assertive manner. Uses language to manipulate, criticize, blame or threaten their partner. Does not listen to or respect what his/her partner says or feels.

Has controlling and possessive behavior. Wants to know everything your partner is doing at all times. Checks your cell phone, social networks or email. Forbids you to see your friends or family. She imposes her tastes or hobbies on you.

She has an unstable and impulsive temperament. Changes moods frequently and for no apparent reason. She goes from euphoria to depression or from tenderness to aggressiveness. Has disproportionate and unpredictable reactions to any situation.

She has a victimizing and dependent attitude. She feels helpless and unable to solve her problems on her own. Constantly seeks help or comfort from her partner. Makes her feel guilty for everything that happens to her or for what she does not do for her.

Lacks empathy and respect. Does not put herself in her partner's shoes or take an interest in her feelings or needs. Does not respect their opinions or decisions. Does not recognize her mistakes or ask for forgiveness.

A toxic woman behaves with her partner in the following ways:

Lies to you about important or trivial things to hide information from you or to get what he wants.

She emotionally blackmails him to do what she wants or not to leave her. She tells him things like "if you love me, you will do this" or "if you leave me, I will kill myself".

Makes you feel guilty for everything you do or don't do. Constantly reproaches you for your failures or absences. She says things like "it's your fault I'm like this" or "you're never there when I need you".

Threatens to leave you, to cheat on you, or to hurt you if you don't comply with her wishes or if you oppose her. She tells her things like "if you don't change, I'm leaving" or "if you leave me, you'll regret it".
